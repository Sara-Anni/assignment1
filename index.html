Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh is emerging as a hub for tech innovation, with startups driving growth in fintech, e-commerce, and IT solutions.

Sustainability

The nation is a global leader in sustainable textile production, advancing eco-friendly practices in the garment industry.

Infrastructure

With projects like the Padma Bridge, Bangladesh is revolutionizing connectivity and boosting economic development.

Empowerment

Bangladesh champions women’s empowerment, with significant strides in gender equality and workforce participation.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Poverty is not created by the poor; it is created by the system we have built, emphasizing the need to address systemic flaws. Bangladesh has shown the world that even the poorest of the poor can work hard to change their own lives when given the right opportunities. Advocating for sustainable solutions, social business is a way to solve problems and create a world of zero poverty, zero unemployment, and zero net carbon emissions. Highlighting the nation’s potential, Bangladesh has the capacity to lead in green technology and renewable energy to combat climate challenges. Furthermore, women are the greatest untapped resource of our society, and empowering them can transform Bangladesh into a prosperous nation.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
